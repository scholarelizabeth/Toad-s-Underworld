To help Toad survive this nightmare press the space key to jump on the evil ghost's heads. You have 4 lives on the top left, and there are flying koopa troopa powerups that make you fly for a short period of time plus a higher jump toad powerup. If the ghost hits you you lose one life. If you lose all 4 lives you get a gameover screen. There are 5 different levels with different faster enemies, different backgrounds, more kills needed, and different music. Lastly there is a final Boss fight. Move left and right until the final boss comes than jump on his head to win and see the end winning screen!!!
